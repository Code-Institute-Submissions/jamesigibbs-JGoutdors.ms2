Our Activities

Here at JG Outdoors we run a wide variety of activities to suit everyone, young and old. From children experiencing their first taste of outdoor adventure, schools looking to enhance their outside learning programme to corporate teams looking to solidify their team and build positive working relationships, not forgetting individuals just wanting to have fun. All of our activities are led by our qualified instructors with each session lasting 1 hour and 30 minutes. Each of our sessions has a maximum capacity of 12 participants, however, sometimes we can accommodate larger groups on prior agreement. Please see each individual activity for clothing requirements.
