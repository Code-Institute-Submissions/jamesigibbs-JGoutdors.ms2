About Us

Welcome to JG Outdoors, a leading outdoor learning venue dedicated to the continuous development of young people.

Our unique heritage and beautiful site features a range of accommodation options, including a Grade I listed calendar house, lodges and camping areas. Through an extensive range of inclusive outdoor activities and tailored programmes, we provide the opportunity for everyone to ‘Experience, Learn and Develop’.

Whether you are looking to book a residential, a short break or visit us for the day, we are accessible to all and our staff are committed to making your visit an unforgettable experience, the adventure starts here……
